About Me

I don’t approach frontend development as surface-level design — I treat it as behavioral engineering. Every interface I build is shaped by intent, timing, and control, where structure guides attention before users consciously recognize it. I work through AI-assisted workflows to accelerate execution, but the logic, flow, and aesthetic judgment remain deliberate and precise. Prompt engineering defines the framework; refinement, editing, and visual restraint define the outcome. My focus is on responsive, SEO-driven, performance-aware experiences that feel effortless on the surface, yet are carefully constructed underneath to retain attention, reduce friction, and influence interaction without noise.
